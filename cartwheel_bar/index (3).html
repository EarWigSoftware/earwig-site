About the bar

A proper local in the centre of Crossmaglen

The Cartwheel sits right on Cardinal O'Fiaich Square in the heart of Crossmaglen, South Armagh. Whether you're dropping in after work, catching live music on the weekend, or joining one of our league nights, there's always a welcome waiting.

We host regular live music sessions, traditional singing nights and events in partnership with An Bonnán Buí CCE, bringing the best of South Armagh's vibrant music scene right to your doorstep.

A place for the community. All are welcome.
