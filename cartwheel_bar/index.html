Céad Míle Fáilte

Your local in the heart of Crossmaglen

A hundred thousand welcomes to The Cartwheel.

Sitting proudly on Cardinal O'Fiach Square, The Cartwheel is one of Crossmaglen's best-loved bars. We're a sports bar and live music venue rolled into one, with a reputation built on a perfectly poured pint and a proper warm welcome.

Whether you're calling in for a quiet Guinness, meeting friends to watch the match, or settling in for a night of live music, you'll always find a good atmosphere and good company waiting at The Cartwheel.

"There are no strangers here,
only friends you haven't met."

What we offer

Everything a good local should be

A Great Pint

Renowned across South Armagh for a perfectly poured pint. Guinness, Harp, Heineken and more, served exactly as they should be.

Live Music

Regular live music nights with the best local acts. Keep an eye on our socials for what's coming up and who's playing.

Live Sport

All the big games on the big screens. GAA, football, racing and more. The best spot in Cross to roar on your team.

Pool & Darts

Pool tables and dartboards ready and waiting. Challenge your mates to a game any night of the week.

Stay in the loop
