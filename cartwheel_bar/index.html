Céad Míle Fáilte

Your local in the heart of Crossmaglen

A hundred thousand welcomes to The Cartwheel.

Sitting proudly on Cardinal O'Fiach Square, The Cartwheel is one of Crossmaglen's best-loved bars. A sports bar and live music venue rolled into one, with a reputation built on a perfectly poured pint and a proper warm welcome.

Whether you're calling in for a quiet Guinness, meeting friends to watch the match, or settling in for a night of music, you'll always find good atmosphere and good company waiting.
