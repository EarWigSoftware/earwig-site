Our story

We believe art and coffee belong together.

Cafés have always been a home for ideas, creativity and connection. We're building that place in Dundalk, somewhere to slow down, look at something beautiful, and meet the people making it. Everything we've saved over the last year is going into this room, and we'd love you in it.

A café, a gallery and a quiet desk, all under one roof.

Four things we're certain about, before we've poured a single cup.

Room for small events

Launches, readings, workshops, open mics. The floor is yours after hours.

A corner for big ideas

Coffee worth the trip

Carefully sourced, properly made. The reason you came, the start of the conversation.

For makers
